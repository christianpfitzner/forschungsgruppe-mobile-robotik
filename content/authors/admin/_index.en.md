Prof. Dr. Christian Pfitzner is a Professor of Mobile Robotics at Technische Hochschule Nürnberg Georg Simon Ohm,
Faculty of Electrical Engineering, Precision Engineering and Information Technology (efi).
He also serves as Director of the Technology Transfer Centre (TTZ) Nürnberger Land.

His research focuses on autonomous robotic systems, drone technology, sensor fusion, and localisation methods for mobile platforms.
